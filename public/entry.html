📷 Whiteboard photo → OCR

Snap a physical whiteboard or a handwritten/printed report. The image is read by the LLM OCR pipeline (whiteboard-ocr.mjs) into structured records — claims, production, downtime, 5W1H — then folded into the cockpit. The cockpit itself stays no-AI; OCR runs as a separate keyed step.

💬 Viber group → records

Long-press chat in Viber → Select → Copy. Paste here, tag the group, save. The LLM (viber-ingest.mjs) extracts orders / payments / claims / deliveries, scoped per group: each role only sees the groups they belong to (set in admin).
